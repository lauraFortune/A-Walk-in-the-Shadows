Kilmainham Gaol

A very interesting building. Very important people in the country were imprisoned there and the visitor can get a sense of what life was like there, you can even enter the cells. Back in the 1800s conditions in Kilmainham Gaol were terrible, and many prisoners perished there. It is said that now their ghosts haunt the place…
